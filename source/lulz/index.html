NOTES:
    - Characters run on US I.
    - Characters join a party with 'earthMer'.
    - Characters will not sell, transfer, or bank items they collect.
    - Characters will heal with hp potions if there are any. They won't buy any.

How to stop:
    1) Run parent.api_call("disconnect_character", {name: "character_id_here"}) in Adventure Land from another character.

How to generate a new auth code & disable old auth codes:
    1) Go to https://adventure.Land
    2) Click on 'Email and Account'
    3) Click on 'Logout Everywhere'
